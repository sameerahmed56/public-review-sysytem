TOP PROJECT

1. Bharatmala: The development of any nation depends on the transportation networks and the ways in which they are being maintained. The same holds true for the development of a huge and populous nation like India. It is a project for connecting major cities across Indian Cost.

TOP PROJECT

2. Sagarmala: Vision of the Sagarmala Programme is to reduce logistics cost for EXIM and domestic trade with minimal infrastructure investment. As part of Sagarmala Programme, more than 610 projects (Cost: Rs. 7.78 Lacs Cr.). It is currently in its first stage of completion.

TOP PROJECT

3. Inland Waterways: Inland Waterways Authority of India (IWAI), anticipating an increase in cargo traffic, plans to purchase 8-10 inland cargo vessels to operate on two key waterways – the Ganga and the Brahmaputra (These are the 2 longest rivers of Indian Subcontinenet).

TOP PROJECT

3. Mumbai Trans Harbour Link, Shivaji Memorial: The proposed Mumbai Trans Harbour Link (‘MTHL') has been planned to facilitate decongestion of the island city by improving connectivity between Island city and main land (Navi Mumbai) and development of Navi Mumbai .
